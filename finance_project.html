Financial Record Project

In this project I'll go deep into my finances since I came to Canada, starting with my first statement, trying to understand a bit better my expenses habits and to prove my abilities as a data analist.

In here we are going to explore all the 6 phases of the data analysis life cycle, going deeply into each one of them.

Step #1 - Ask

To better understand the problem we want to solve in this project I've got a few questions, such as follows:

  • What were my key expenses from August 2021 to October 2023?
  • How much has my revenue increased since my first job as a supervisor in a fast food to becoming a bartender?

To answer these questions I've got to get access of my e-estatements from my online banking, but this is another step of the data analysis life cycle called prepare.

Step #2 - Prepare

This phase of the analysis is responsible to but not restricted to aquiring the data we are going to work with. I've got the records for my bank account from 08/23/2021 to 10/23/2023, computing a total of 27 records altogether.

Milestone Hit

The e-Statement files were in pdf format and the first idea I had to convert it to usable data was to read the PDF files in python using the tabula library, however, it wasn't able to read all the pages from the e-Statements.

After deciding to ask chat-gpt how to solve this problem, I used an online pdf converter to xlsx, which revealled to be a much easier way of accomplishing this task.
